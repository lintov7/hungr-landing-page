Hungr

Homely food daily

Hungr helps to connect customers with tiffin service provider. Insead of ordering food daily, customer can subscribe to a tiffin service provider and get the food delivered where they want.
You can install the applications in android and iPhone

Screenshots

Features

Find Restaurants

Find near by restaurants which provide tiffin services

Book Tiffin

Using the application users can easily view different menu book tiffin service

Home Delivery

Users can get the food items delivered to their home if tiffin provider supports it
